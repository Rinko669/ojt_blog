FEBRUARY 2, 2024

On February 2, we were called to attend an orientation for those scheduled to be deployed at the University Library. We discussed several important topics. First, Ma'am Glenda outlined the expectations and explained why we chose to conduct our OJT at the University Library. Ma'am Lai, who was in charge of assigning tasks, and Sir Michael, who supervises us and delegates tasks, also contributed to the discussion.

Later, we participated in a dry run to familiarize ourselves with the tasks and received briefings on other possible assignments that would be given during our OJT period..

Here are some tasks I completed:

  • Practiced scanning some thesis documents
  • Assisted in fixing the computer setup.

Since it was still a dry run with limited tasks, we spent the rest of the day getting accustomed to the environment..
