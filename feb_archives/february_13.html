FEBRUARY 13, 2024

On this day, February 13,we spent the day revisiting and re-scanning several theses that required further attention. This process was necessary to ensure that all documents were accurately digitized and properly archived. Although it was a repetitive task, it played a crucial role in maintaining the quality and integrity of our digital records. Each thesis represents a significant amount of work, so ensuring its correct digital preservation is essential for future reference and academic use.
